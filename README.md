# dominion-forger
Dominion Forger is a customizable kingdom generator and card browser for Dominion. It allows players to search, filter, and explore the full card pool while generating kingdoms that match specific rules and preferences.

Features include:

Advanced card filtering by type, mechanics, and attributes

Text search for card abilities and keywords

Customizable kingdom randomization rules

Card rating with adjustable bias (love / like / dislike / hate)

Locked cards for partial rerolls

Automatic setup reminders for tokens, landscapes, and non-supply piles

Local storage for ratings, preferences, and owned cards

Instead of relying on pure randomness, Dominion Forger lets you shape the probability space and forge kingdoms that fit your play style.
