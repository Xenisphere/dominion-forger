# DRAW / HAND CONTROL
draw            - gives +Cards or otherwise increases hand size
cantrip         - gives at least +1 Card AND +1 Action
discard         - discards cards from hand (self or others)
scry            - looks at/reorders top of deck
topdeck         - gains or places cards onto top of deck

# ACTIONS / FLOW
actions         - gives +Actions
village         - gives +2 or more Actions
terminal        - Action card that does NOT give +Actions

# ECONOMY
coin            - gives +Coin
buy             - gives +Buy
payload         - produces money or buys (coin OR buy)
gain            - gains cards
cost_control    - modifies costs (reduce or increase)

# TRASHING / DECK CONTROL
trash           - trashes cards
self_trash      - trashes itself
trash_benefit   - trashes for a benefit (gain, draw, coin, etc.)
filtering       - lets you choose from cards (look at, pick, etc.)

# INTERACTION
attack          - affects other players (any "each other player")
junking         - gives bad cards (Curse, Ruins, Copper)
hand_attack     - disrupts hand (discard down, reveal hand, etc.)
deck_attack     - affects top of opponent deck

# TRIGGERS
on_gain         - has "when you gain" effect
on_buy          - has "when you buy" effect

# SPECIAL MECHANICS
duration        - effect persists to next turn
multi_turn      - explicitly affects multiple turns
vp_tokens       - gives victory tokens
alt_vp          - grants or modifies victory points

# SETUP / EXTRAS
extra_cards     - uses non-supply cards (Spoils, Horses, etc.)
setup           - requires special setup beyond normal kingdom

# DERIVED (COMPUTED)
engine_piece    - contributes to engine building (draw OR village OR trash)
payload_piece   - provides scoring/output (coin OR buy)
terminal_draw   - has draw but is terminal (draw AND NOT actions)
non_terminal_draw - has draw AND actions
gain_engine     - gains cards AND supports engine (gain AND actions)
trash_engine    - trashes AND supports engine (trash AND actions)

# FALLBACK
utility         - assigned if no other tags apply
